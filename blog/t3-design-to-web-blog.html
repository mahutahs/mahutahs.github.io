What a responsive site is, and why responsiveness is important

Well a responsive site is a website that responds to how you view it. wether it be by laptop, cell phone tablet ect. It is important because it allows the user to view the website on their different gadgets


What mobile first design is, and why it's important

Mobile first design is a approach to responsive design. In short it focuses on design for smaller screens first, then add more features and content for bigger and bigger screens. This is important because it forces you to design something but thinking about how the mobile site works before making the bigger site


What frameworks are, and their pros and cons
